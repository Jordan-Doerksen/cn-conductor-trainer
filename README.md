# CN Conductor Trainer — Grande Prairie Yard

A browser-based training tool to rebuild CN freight-conductor skills on CN's **Grande Prairie Yard**, grounded in the **January 2025 Canadian Rail Operating Rules (CROR)**.

**Open `index.html`** (or visit the live site) — it's the hub that launches every module. No install; everything runs in the browser.

## Modules
- **Rule Drills** — CROR question reps: switches & derails, securement, signals, radio.
- **Switching Puzzle** — plan and work cuts on the real yard: dig-outs, tight-room (capacity), build-an-outbound, leave-it-clean, plus a random mixed mode. Every puzzle's par is verified by a solver.
- **Yard Board** — the Grande Prairie yard, traced from the operating diagram, interactive (tracks + switches).
- **Radio & Steps** — a guided "back to a joint" coupling: positive ID, the car-then-feet count, stretch, set & center, cut the air in, release.
- **Classify the Cut** — quick scenarios on the GP kick rules.

## Built on
- January 2025 CROR (rule numbers cited throughout).
- CN Grande Prairie Yard operating-practices diagram (Sept 2020), traced into the board.

## Disclaimer
Training aid only. The official **CROR**, CN **special instructions**, and **General Operating Instructions** always govern. Some radio phrasing reflects operational/CN practice and is flagged as such, not as a rule.
