Radio & Steps — Back to a Joint
Guided · car counts to feet, couple, verify, protect, cut the air, release
Engine CN 2204 backs its cut up to couple onto a car on AS73. You're on the ground, in the clear, watching the tail car and the joint. Count it down — cars, then feet — verify the coupling, protect, couple the air, release.

Radio

Why this call

Make your first call to begin — identify the engine, railway initials and number digit by digit, and wait for the engineer to answer up.
CN GOI = operational practice, not a CROR rule. Green lines are the engineer; he repeats the car counts and calls the air to the tail.
